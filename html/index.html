About WikiVis

With how data is seen in every facet of life ranging from the connection between Twitch streamers to the amount of showers each CS major partakes in, we hope to create a visualization that allows everyday people to see those connections and create something beautiful from it. There exist many areas where data can be used but one interesting data visualization which we have not seen is the connection between Wikipedia articles. Inspired by games like Wikirace, we are creating a new way, more beautiful to visualize data. And hey, what if we make it crazier and overlay these connections on a 3 dimensional plane?

All you have to do is enter the title of the Wikipedia article you are interested in (case sensitive) and click the Visualize button. Then you'll see a graph of articles visualized as spherical nodes, all of which are connected based on how the articles are linked.

Application Controls

  • Spacebar: Play/Pause visualization animation
  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Navigate between nodes
  • Enter: Go to article corresponding to current node
  • Left Mouse Button: Click and drag to move visualization around
