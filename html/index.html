About WikiVis

Have you ever wanted to see how Wikipedia articles connect to each other? Well, now you can! Introducing WikiVis! A new, elegant way to visualize data. In 3D! Enter a word and see where it takes you!

All you have to do is enter the title of the Wikipedia article you are interested in (case sensitive) and click the Visualize button. Then you'll see a graph of articles visualized as spherical nodes, all of which are connected based on how the articles are linked.

Application Controls

  • Spacebar: Play/Pause visualization animation
  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Navigate between nodes
  • Enter: Go to article corresponding to current node
  • Left Mouse Button: Click and drag to move visualization around
